# Color System in Themes

Themes offers a range of **color scales**, each with light, dark, and alpha variants. These scales are powered by **Colors**.

## Accents

The **accent color** is the most dominant color in your theme. It is used for primary buttons, links, and other interactive elements.  
You can specify the `accentColor` directly on the `Theme` component:

```jsx
<Theme accentColor="indigo">
  <MyApp />
</Theme>
```
